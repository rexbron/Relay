Search messages

Search your conversations

Press ⌘G or click the search field in the sidebar. Type your query to perform a full-text search across your rooms. Results appear in the sidebar with the room name, sender, and a message snippet.

Navigate to a result

Click a search result to open the room and scroll to the matching message. The message is briefly highlighted.

Filter rooms

Use the search bar at the top of the sidebar to filter the room list by name. This is separate from message search and works within the current space.

Note: Message search is performed server-side by your homeserver. Search results may vary depending on your homeserver's search capabilities.
