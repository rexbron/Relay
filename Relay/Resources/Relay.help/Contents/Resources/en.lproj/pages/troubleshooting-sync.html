Troubleshoot sync issues

Offline and unreachable banners

Relay shows a banner in the sidebar when connectivity is lost:

  • "Network Offline": your Mac has no network connection (check Wi-Fi or Ethernet)
  • "Server Unreachable": your network is working but Relay can't reach the homeserver (the server may be down or experiencing issues)

Missing or stale rooms

If rooms are missing from your sidebar or show outdated messages:

  • Wait a moment; Relay syncs in the background and rooms may appear after initial sync completes
  • Switch to a new room or space; this triggers a room list refresh
  • As a last resort, choose Relay > Clear Cache to force a full re-sync

Messages not appearing

If new messages aren't showing up:

  • Check the offline banner; you may be disconnected
  • Scroll to the bottom of the timeline; the "scroll to bottom" button appears when you're viewing history
  • Check Settings > Behavior to make sure "Always load newest messages" is enabled if you want to start at the latest messages

Timeline stuck loading

If the timeline shows a loading spinner that doesn't resolve:

  • Switch to another room and back
  • If the problem persists, clear the cache

Clear cache

Choose Relay > Clear Cache from the menu bar. This removes all locally cached data and forces Relay to re-sync from the homeserver. You remain signed in.

Note: After clearing the cache, the initial sync may take a while depending on how many rooms you've joined. During this time, rooms will gradually appear in the sidebar.
