Troubleshoot sign-in issues

"Invalid Matrix ID" error

Make sure your Matrix ID follows the format @username:server.org. The server part must be a valid hostname.

Wrong homeserver URL

If your homeserver uses a non-standard URL (different from the server part of your Matrix ID), expand the Advanced section on the sign-in screen and enter the correct homeserver URL.

OAuth/SSO sign-in doesn't complete

If the browser window opens but sign-in doesn't finish:

  • Check that your browser isn't blocking popups from Relay
  • Try signing in with a password instead, if your homeserver supports it
  • Check if your homeserver's SSO provider is experiencing issues

"Server unreachable" during sign-in

Verify that your homeserver is online and that your network connection is working. Try opening your homeserver's URL in a web browser.

Session restore fails

If Relay doesn't restore your session after a restart:

  • Your session token may have been invalidated by the homeserver
  • Try signing in again with your credentials
  • If the problem persists, choose Clear Cache from the Relay menu to reset local data, then sign in again

Clear cache

If you experience persistent sign-in problems, choose Relay > Clear Cache from the menu bar. This deletes local data and forces a fresh sync. You will not be signed out.

Important: Clearing the cache does not delete your account or messages. It only removes locally cached data. Relay will re-sync with your homeserver after clearing.
