Use reactions and mentions

Add a reaction

  1. Right-click a message and choose Add Reaction, or hover over a message and click the reaction button.
  2. The emoji picker appears.
  3. Click an emoji to toggle it on or off.

Recently used emoji

The reaction picker tracks your recently used emoji for quick access.

Mention a user

  1. Type @ in the compose bar to open the mention autocomplete.
  2. Use the arrow keys to navigate the list.
  3. Press Return to insert the mention.

The mention appears as a styled pill in the compose bar and timeline.

Note: Mentions notify the mentioned user even if they have the room muted, depending on their notification settings.
