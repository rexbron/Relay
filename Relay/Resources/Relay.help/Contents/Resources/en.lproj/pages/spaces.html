Organize rooms with spaces

What are spaces?

Spaces are a way to group related rooms together, similar to folders or workspaces. A space can contain rooms and other sub-spaces.

Create a space

  1. Choose File > New Space (⇧⌘N).
  2. Enter a name and optional topic.
  3. Choose Public or Private visibility.

The space rail

The vertical icon rail on the left edge of the window lets you switch between spaces. It includes a Home button (showing all rooms) and a Create Space button.

Add rooms to a space

  1. Open the space detail view.
  2. Click Add Room.
  3. Select from your joined rooms.

Create sub-spaces

Inside a space, you can create child spaces for further organization. Use the breadcrumb bar to navigate the space hierarchy.

Remove a room from a space

Right-click a room in the space detail view and choose Remove from Space.

Invite to a space

Open the space detail view and choose Invite to invite users by Matrix ID.

Leave a space

Right-click the space in the space rail. A confirmation sheet shows the child rooms with checkboxes, allowing you to leave them simultaneously.
