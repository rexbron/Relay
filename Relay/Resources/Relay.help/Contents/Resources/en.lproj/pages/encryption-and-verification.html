Privacy & Security

Encryption and session verification

About end-to-end encryption

Relay uses Matrix's end-to-end encryption (E2EE) to secure messages in encrypted rooms. Only participants in the room can read the messages. Encryption is enabled per-room when the room is created.

Session verification

When you sign in on a new device, you need to verify your session to access encrypted message history. A verification banner appears at the bottom of the sidebar until your session is verified.

Verify with another device

If you have another device already signed in:

  1. Click the verification banner, or go to Settings > Sessions and click Verify Session.
  2. Accept the verification request on your other device.
  3. Compare the 7 emoji shown on both devices.
  4. If they match, confirm on both devices.

Verify with a recovery key

If you don't have another device available:

  1. Click Use Recovery Key in the verification sheet.
  2. Enter your security/recovery key.
  3. Your session is verified and encrypted history becomes accessible.

Encryption status

The Security tab in the room inspector shows whether encryption is enabled for a room. Once enabled, encryption cannot be turned off.

View session info

Go to Settings > Sessions to see all your signed-in devices, including device name, ID, last seen time, and IP address. Settings > Encryption shows your session's verification, key backup, and account recovery status.

Warning: Keep your recovery key in a safe place. If you lose access to all your devices and your recovery key, you will not be able to read your encrypted message history.
