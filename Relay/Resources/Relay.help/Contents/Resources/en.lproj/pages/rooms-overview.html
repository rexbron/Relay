Create and join rooms

Create a room

  1. Choose File > New Room (⌘N).
  2. Enter a name and optional topic.
  3. Choose Public or Private visibility.
  4. Toggle end-to-end encryption on or off.
  5. For public rooms, you can set a room address (#alias:server).

Join a room by address

  1. Choose File > Join Room (⌘J).
  2. Enter a room ID (!room:server) or alias (#alias:server).

Browse the room directory

Choose File > Room Directory (⇧⌘D) to browse your homeserver's public room directory. Search for rooms and preview their details before joining.

Room previews

When browsing the directory or following a matrix.to link, you can preview room details—name, topic, avatar, and member count—before committing to join.

Accept or decline invitations

Pending invitations appear in the Invites section at the top of the sidebar. Click an invitation to preview the room, then accept or decline.

Leave a room

Right-click a room in the sidebar and choose Leave, or swipe left. A confirmation dialog appears before the room is removed.

Pin rooms

Right-click a room and choose Pin to keep it at the top of the sidebar in a Pinned section.

Tip: Use Quick Switch (⌘K) to jump to any room instantly without scrolling through the sidebar.
